About

Hi, I am a computer vision researcher. I completed my Master degree in Computer Engineering at Chonnam National Univesity in Fall 2019, advised by Prof. Guee-Sang Lee. I also led a project on 4D Printing and finished my Master degree in Material Engineering under supervision of Prof. Joo-Yong Kim at Soongsil University in 2018. Before that, I was a Lecturer at Van Lang University and participated in writing some technical drawing & CAD books.

I am mainly interested in the intersection of machine learning and computer vision to contributedly build machine that able to engage in physical world. Now I am focusing on a wide range of topics, including deep generative model, minimal supervision, zero-shot learning for computer vision tasks, and 3D vision for robotics.
