Son Thai Ly

I am a researcher at Chonnam National University, South Korea. I completed my Master degree in Computer Science at the same institution in Fall 2019, advised by Prof. Guee-Sang Lee. I also led a project on 4D Printing and finished my Master degree in Material Engineering under supervision of Prof. Joo-Yong Kim at Soongsil University in 2018. Without the guidance and support of these two Professors, I wouldn't have found my academic pathway, and I will always be grateful for that.

Before that, I was a Lecturer at Van Lang University and participated in writing some technical drawing & CAD books (these and this in Vietnamese though) because I enjoy transfering knowledge to others.

Other than academic pursuits, I love doing pretty much every brain-needed sport because it helps my thinking.

               

Research Interests
Although my recent works are application researches, I am primarily interested in the intesection of representation learning and deep generative model. More specifically, I focus on developing efficient algorithms that not only exploit useful patterns on specific in-the-wild large-scale datasets, but also perform well on little and unseen data. I am also interested in optimization and computer vision.
